Froakie - Bubble Frog Pokémon - Water

It secretes flexible yet delicate bubbles from its chest and back that reduce the damage it would otherwise take when attacked. Despite its happy-go-lucky air, it keeps a watchful eye on its surroundings.

Weak Against:
Grass | Electric

Strong Against:
Fire | Ground | Rock

Chespin - Spiky Nut Pokémon - Grass

A thick shell of wood and quills covers its head and back, not letting a direct hit from a truck even faze it. The quills on its head are usually soft, becoming so hard and sharp when flexed that they can pierce rock.

Weak Against:
Fire | Poison

Strong Against:
Water | Rock | Ground

Fennekin - Fire Fox Pokémon - Fire

As it walks, it munches on a twig in place of a snack, filling it with energy. It intimidates opponents by puffing hot air out of its roomy ears that make the air hotter than 390 degrees Fahrenheit.

Weak Against:
Water | Rock | Ground

Strong Against:
Grass | Ice | Bug
